A Crowdsourced DNS of Things

Moeco is built to become a DNS of things — a platform integrating various connectivity standards and connecting billions of devices across the globe. Moeco uses crowdsourcing to gain coverage and help businesses effortlessly adopt IoT technology in a most cost-effective way.

Crowdsourced Network

Moeco brings together existing networks and private gateway owners by crowdsourcing its infrastructure — data from the sensors can be transferred using any smartphoneor compa- tible gateway (even home-based).

Permissionless Access

The platform allows users to collect and trans- fer any type of data from any sensor anywhere in the world. Adding a new sensor is easy and permissionless — just add its ID using a website.

Motivation Tool

The platform takes care of payment and billing processes, and ensures exact data delivery. Businesses owning sensors pay for connectivity and the gateway owners receive money for each connection made through their gateway.

Roles

Moeco makes the community its IoT infrastructure. All of the user types operate within the blockchain-based platform.

Device Manufacturer

With Moeco device manufacturers can easily become global service pro- viders. Your IoT devices will work anywhere without limitations or the need to for complicated contracts with carriers.

Gate Owner

Anyone can provide IoT connectivity and earn money with a Moeco gateway! Just set up our open-source LoRa WAN compatible gateway, or even install our app on your Bluetooth LE-enabled smartphone and start making money right away! Fixing IoT One Transaction at a Time

Moeco is set to become the DNS of things. By leveraging the benefits of a multiple blockchain system Moeco makes per-transaction fees possible with MOE tokens.

Become an internet provider for things. Share your traffic with IoT devices & get tokens.

Moe Tokens

Our token economy is based on the turnover of MOE tokens between consumers of connectivity (devices owned by users) and providers (gateways owned by users).

Tokens provide a native payment solution for connections. They make per-transaction fees possible.

Moe tokens represent the sum total of connection. As the number of con- nections grows, so does the token value.

Tokens are infinitely divisible, accom- modating network growth and expansion along the product development cycle.
